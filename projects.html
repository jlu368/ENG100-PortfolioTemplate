NGIC Helmet Re-engineering Project

As a high school senior, I participated with a team of 3 in the Next Generations Innovators Challenge. Our goal was to redesign the football helmet to help reduce and prevent concussions. In this project, I did considerable research on the subjet, made CAD models, and performed extensive testing on our ideas. We competed in a competition with 40-45 teams from around the state of Illinois and placed 4th.
